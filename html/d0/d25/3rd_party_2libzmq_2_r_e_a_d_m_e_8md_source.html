7 ## Welcome
8 
9 The ZeroMQ lightweight messaging kernel is a library which extends the
10 standard socket interfaces with features traditionally provided by
11 specialised messaging middleware products. ZeroMQ sockets provide an
12 abstraction of asynchronous message queues, multiple messaging patterns,
13 message filtering (subscriptions), seamless access to multiple transport
14 protocols and more.
