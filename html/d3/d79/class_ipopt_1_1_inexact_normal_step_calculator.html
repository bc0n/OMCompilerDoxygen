virtual bool Ipopt::InexactNormalStepCalculator::ComputeNormalStep ( SmartPtr< Vector > &  normal_x,
SmartPtr< Vector > &  normal_s 
)
pure virtual

Method for computing the normal step. The computed step is returned as normal_x and normal_s, for the x and s variables, respectively. These quantities are not slack-scaled. If the step cannot be computed, this method returns false.
